Luke Hearne, PhD

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Clinical Brain Networks group, QIMR Berghofer. I also serve as a scientific advisor at QNC, a not-for-profit clinic that provides MRI-based personalised Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation for treatment resistant depression. I'm currently funded by an NHMRC Emerging Leadership fellow grant.

I am interested in the large-scale functional and structural organisation of the brain, and how it relates to cognition and psychiatric illness. I use a combination of neuroimaging, computational modeling and brain stimulation to investigate these questions.
